About US

We are Chitralaya,

An End-to-End Virtual Production House

Chitralaya is your ultimate destination for all things Photography, Design, and Media. With a passion for storytelling and an eye for detail, we specialize in capturing life’s most precious moments – from grand weddings and intimate gatherings to stunning product shots and intricate macro photography.

We don’t stop at just clicking pictures; we craft experiences. Our expertise extends to post-production, where we transform raw images into polished masterpieces, ensuring every frame tells a story that’s as unique as you are.

At Chitralaya, we believe memories deserve to be celebrated. That’s why we go beyond photography to offer custom framing services, turning your cherished moments into timeless art. Whether it’s a candid laugh, a tearful embrace, or the intricate details of a product – we’re here to make every shot unforgettable.

Let’s create magic together – because your memories deserve nothing but the best.
